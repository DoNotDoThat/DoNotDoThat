We are a group of 4 teenagers who had an idea of creating a less threatening internet for the younger generation. And here is our story.

Early one Monday morning 4(-1) young and inexperienced coding pioneers walked into the BBC building in Birmingham with the aim of making the internet a safer place for all contrasting persona's.

With our aim it was clear that the easiest place to start would be social networking, we decided on the teen phenomenon that is of course Twitter. And so the legend began.
