The Big Idea

Our big idea is to make Twitter a less threatening place, due to the fact Twitter allows you to message whoever you want at any time without face to face contact. These "Keyboard Warriors" often without realising start to bully individuals.

This has personally affected one of our groups members Nic, he was attacked by a cyber bully on his youtube account. However Twitter is the new social network on the rise so it was very important to Nic that we made Twitter a safer less threatening place.

Twitter has no language/profanity/foulness blocker and with many young users constantly using Twitter they are exposed and vulnerable that's where Do Not do That comes in! Do Not Do That messages user Tweeting tweets we find inappropriate and replies to the tweet they should do that and reports the data to our website so we and others can monitor how many threats are being tweeted and what type.
